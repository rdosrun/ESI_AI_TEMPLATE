# Stakeholder Discovery Template

## Business Problem

- What decision or workflow needs improvement?
- Who owns the current process?
- What happens today when the process fails?
- What is the cost of doing nothing?

## Users

- Which teams will use the AI workflow?
- What roles will submit questions or documents?
- Who reviews answers for quality?
- Who approves release to a wider audience?

## Data

- What documents or structured data should the AI use?
- Who owns the data?
- Are there sensitive, regulated, or customer-identifying fields?
- How often does the data change?
- What content should be excluded?

## Success Criteria

- What does a good answer look like?
- What answer quality threshold is acceptable for a pilot?
- What KPI will prove the workflow is worth expanding?
- What failure rate would stop the pilot?

## Workflow Fit

- Where should this AI workflow appear in the existing process?
- Does it need to connect to an LMS, SOP repository, CRM, ticketing system, or data warehouse?
- When should a human expert take over?
- What audit trail is required?
