## Scoring

Score each category from 1 to 5:

- 1: Does not meet pilot needs.
- 2: Significant gaps.
- 3: Acceptable for limited pilot.
- 4: Strong fit with manageable gaps.
- 5: Strong fit for pilot and production path.
